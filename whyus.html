Empowering Futures: Transforming Education and Making It Accessible

Why Us?

Our mission towards equitable futures

At AceAct, our mission is clear: to develop the first entirely free, comprehensive ACT preparation course designed exclusively for high school students. Our goal is to democratize education and eliminate barriers, ensuring that every student regardless of financial constraints has access to learning resources. By empowering students with the tools they need to excel on the ACT, we aim to level the playing field for students from all backgrounds to achieve their fill potential.

All-encompassing resources to coach our students

At AceACT, we strive to provide premium ACT study materials, practice tests, and all the tools necessary for thorough ACT preparation. Through a variety of resources, including practice exams, instructional videos, and specialized tools, AceACT works to holistically cover all subjects. Our comprehensive approach ensures that students receive well-rounded preparation, equipping them with the knowledge and skills they need to tackle every section of the ACT with confidence and competence.

Because Every Student Deserves a Bright Future with ACT Excellence

We transform each student's potential into achievement through our ACT Success initiative. By empowering students and unlocking opportunities through our free classes, we pave the way for bright futures. Our commitment to providing accessible education means that every student has the chance to succeed, regardless of their circumstances. With a strong focus on individual growth and academic excellence, we help students build a solid foundation for their future endeavors, ensuring that they can pursue their dreams and aspirations without limitations.

Who are we?

AceAct is a project of CloudCores Group, composed of dedicated individuals with diverse skills united by a common goal: making a meaningful impact on people's lives. We are a team of ambitious students driven by a passion for helping others, equipped with the skills to achieve our objectives. CloudCores, a software development company, is deeply committed to serving others and harnessing technology for positive change. We prioritize the well-being of our employees and users alike, advocating for their needs and aspirations.
